Features


No bleeding or ghosting.

With ultra thick 160gsm pages, these journals are perfect for any fountain pen, marker, or paint you want to use. It will never show through the page.


Lies flat, even on the first page.

Each .5 cm dot grid page is bound by hand and lays flat for a flawless, studio-quality look every time.


Never lose your place.

Each notebook comes with two ribbon placeholders, so you'll never have trouble finding your page.
